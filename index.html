Who We Are

The CAM Chamber Singers are comprised of local vocal music artists that sing professionally throughout the communities of Metro Detroit; members are drawn from the choristers of the Michigan Opera Theatre and paid choral scholars. This ensemble performs choral masterworks and features new works by living composers. The Community Chorus is a choral ensemble open to community members that are passionate about the choral art. The choir enrolls a diverse membership, and they perform varied works.

Want to Join Us?

The CAM pursues engaging projects that educate and inspire. When students hear and collaborate with highly-skilled, professional singers, the aesthetic experience is unmatched. Choral collaborations inspire performers and audiences, contributing beauty and shared community.

Help Support Us

As with any new organization, the CAM financial needs are great. The organization is seeking grassroots support. In order to perform the 2020 projects, income is needed. That is, 2020 projects are currently underfunded. The Choral Artists of Michigan is a 501(c)(3) organization, so we will provide donation letters at the end of the year. Music Patron donations allow a concert venue, event insurance, print music, soloists, instrumentalists, publicity, and educational outreach.

As a supporter, you will get your name on our “Support” pages. Music Patrons (name in the program) -- $55. Gold Patrons (name in the program) -- $75 or above

We will recognize your partnership in the printed program beginning at $55. Suggested donations are $55, $75, $150, or $500.

Business Sponsors

By partnering with the CAM, your business will

  • Establish association with a local, regionally recognized vocal music ensemble.
  • Raise your community profile, visibility, and philanthropic vision.
  • Make vocal music accessible to new and young audiences.
  • Provide opportunity for children to succeed, achieve, and contribute to our community through the power of music.
Recognition and Reach

In addition, our partnership cultivates a relationship where your business is recognized. Our reach includes concerts at local performing arts centers. Our strategy includes a biweekly email update, Facebook presence, Instagram, Twitter, mailings, and community announcements. We perform concerts and festivals, including the Orion Chamber Music Society String Camp, masterwork classics, new music, and educational outreach in Southeast Michigan.
